Charlotte Mason in Minnesota

Welcome!

We hope this site will cultivate more community among Minnesota families and educators who value Charlotte Mason’s educational philosophy. Please add your homeschool, co-op, nature group, microschool, book club, or other CM-related group to the Minnesota map by registering below!


A note about privacy: Your personal contact information will not be publicly visible. Your name, kids’ ages, and city of residence will be visible if you choose to add that information to this map. If someone wishes to contact you, they will send a blind message through this site. If you choose to reply to that message, you may decide to share your email address or additional contact information with the inquirer.
